History of Giant Lantern Festival

The festival started from simple "parol" traditions during Misas de Gallo (dawn masses) and evolved into a grand event showcasing engineering, artistry, and Christmas spirit.

Art and Technology

Today's lanterns use complex rotors, LED technology, and thousands of lights, creating animated scenes choreographed with music and stunning precision.
