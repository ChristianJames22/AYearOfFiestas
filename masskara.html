Highlights of MassKara Festival

The main highlights are the Street Dancing Competition, the Electric MassKara (a night parade of illuminated floats and dancers), beauty pageants, concerts, food fairs, and colorful mask contests.

History of MassKara

MassKara started in 1980 during a time of crisis in Bacolod. The festival was conceived as a way to uplift spirits by celebrating life through smiles and festivities.

Symbolism of Masks

The colorful smiling masks represent the resilience and positive outlook of Bacolodnons, even during hardships and struggles.

MassKara Today

Today, MassKara is one of the most awaited festivals in the Philippines, promoting Bacolod as a top tourist destination and the ultimate city of smiles.
